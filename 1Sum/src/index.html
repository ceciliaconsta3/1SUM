1Sum

1SUM is an unobtrusive way to get a daily glimpse into the progress of your remote workers/team members.

Here's how it works. Write a quick note about the task you're currently working on. 1Sum will save all of these blurbs for you and at the end of the day you can make a neat little summary, sort of like a Twitter update, and post up for your team.

This way, you can keep note your progress along the way without forgetting the minor yet oh-so-important details. And like a real sticky, only you can see your scattered notes and thought process.

Login to start saving your blurbs and to see what your invisible team is up to.
